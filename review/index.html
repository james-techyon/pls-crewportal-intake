INDEPENDENT CONTRACTOR AGREEMENT

This Independent Contractor Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between Prestige Labor Solutions ("Company") and the undersigned contractor ("Contractor").

1. INDEPENDENT CONTRACTOR STATUS

Contractor acknowledges and agrees that Contractor is an independent contractor and not an employee of Company. Contractor shall have no authority to bind Company or to hold out to any third party that Contractor is an employee or agent of Company.

2. SERVICES

Contractor agrees to provide audio, video, lighting, management, or technical support services as requested by Company on a project-by-project basis.

3. COMPENSATION

Contractor will be compensated at agreed-upon rates for each project. Payment will be made via direct deposit within 30 days of invoice submission and approval.

4. TAXES

Contractor is responsible for all federal, state, and local taxes. Company will not withhold any taxes from Contractor's compensation. Contractor agrees to provide a completed W-9 form.

5. INSURANCE

Contractor is responsible for maintaining appropriate insurance coverage, including general liability and professional liability insurance.

6. CONFIDENTIALITY

Contractor agrees to maintain the confidentiality of all proprietary information received from Company or its clients.

7. TERMINATION

Either party may terminate this Agreement at any time with written notice.

8. GOVERNING LAW

This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the state where services are performed.
